This document formally maps the alignment and misalignment between:

- Apple’s stated identity and values,  
- Apple’s accessibility mission,  
- Apple’s human‑rights policy,  
- Apple’s obligations under the UN Guiding Principles on Business & Human Rights (UNGPs),

# **VI. UNGP HRD‑Specific Obligations Layer**

### **UNGP Requirements for Corporations**
Under UNGPs, corporations must:

- identify HRDs as a **high‑risk group**,  
- ensure **non‑retaliation**,  
- provide **safe channels**,  
- ensure **non‑discriminatory access to remedy**,  
- avoid actions that **silence**, **isolate**, or **harm** HRDs.

### **Crossmap Misalignment**
Apple’s response:

- silenced communication,  
- provided no safe channel,  
- offered no remedy,  
- ignored HRD status,  
- increased cognitive load,  
- increased isolation.

This is a **direct violation** of HRD‑specific UNGP obligations.
